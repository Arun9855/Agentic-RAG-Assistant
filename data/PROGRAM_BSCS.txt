Title: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science: Requirements

Total Units: 128 units minimum.
Composition Requirement: WRIT 150 (4 units) and WRIT 340 (3 units).
General Education: 24 units.
Pre-Major: 30 units (Calculus I, II, III; Linear Algebra; Basic Science sequence - 2 courses; ENGR 102).
CS Core Requirements: CSCI 102 (2), 103 (4), 104 (4), 170 (4), 201 (4), 270 (4), 310 (4), 350 (4), 353 (4), 356 (4), 360 (4), 401 (4) or 404 (4).
Technical Electives: 12 units required.
Co-requisites: Some CSCI courses require concurrent enrollment in lab sections (L) or co-requisite math courses.
