Title: USC Academic Catalog: Grading System and Policies

- Minimum grade for Computer Science core courses: A grade of C (2.0) or better is required for CSCI 102L, CSCI 103, CSCI 170, CSCI 104, and CSCI 201.
- Repetition: A student can repeat a course only if the initial grade was C- or lower. A student can only repeat a core CSCI course once.
- GPA requirement: 2.0 cumulative GPA at USC and for all upper-division major courses.
- Course count: Students cannot take more than 18 units per term without permission.
- Residency: At least 32 units must be completed at USC.
